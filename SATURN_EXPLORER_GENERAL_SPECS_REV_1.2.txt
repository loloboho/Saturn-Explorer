================================================================================
FUSION-POWERED DEEP SPACE EXPLORATION VESSEL
General Specifications & Capabilities
Rev. 1.2 - November 2025
================================================================================

OVERVIEW

This fusion-powered spacecraft represents a generational leap in deep space
exploration capability, designed for long-duration crewed missions to the
outer solar system. The vessel combines continuous-thrust fusion propulsion
with rotating artificial gravity habitat, comprehensive life support systems,
and dual surface exploration landers.

================================================================================
CONSTRUCTION & ASSEMBLY
================================================================================

Assembly Location:            Earth orbit
Construction Method:          Modular orbital assembly
Key Constraint:               2-meter airlock (post-closure access)

Construction Sequence:
1. Aft habitat sphere sections with integrated shelter
2. Rotating habitat ring (built in place)
3. Large components (>2m) installed before sphere closure
4. Sphere completion and sealing
5. Final outfitting through airlock
6. Integration of spine, propulsion, and aft sections

Special Features:
• Welding: Electron beam, laser, friction stir (orbital methods)
• Utilities: Accessible from exterior (maintenance during mission)
• Modular Design: Components sized for orbital assembly
• Heritage: ISS construction experience applicable
